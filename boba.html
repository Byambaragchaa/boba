Boba(also knowns as bubble tea, pearl milk tea, bubble milk tea)

Boba is a tea based drink that originated in Taiwan back in the early 1980's.

The boba tea has many varients and different flavors, but normally consists of some sorts of tea along side a chewy tapioca balls.And it's most popular varients are pearl black milk tea, made with traditional tapioca chewy balls and sweet black milk tea.
